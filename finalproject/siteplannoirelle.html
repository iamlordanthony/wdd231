Noirelle Makeup - Site Plan

Prepared by Lord Anthony Senanu

Site Name

Noirelle Makeup

The name "Noirelle" represents elegance, sophistication, and inclusivity in beauty. It emphasizes makeup designed for all skin tones, with a focus on luxury yet accessible beauty. Optional domain: noirellemakeup.com

Site Purpose

Noirelle Makeup aims to provide customers with access to premium quality beauty products, tutorials, and tips. The site will include product listings, customer reviews, membership opportunities, and makeup inspiration for diverse audiences.

Scenarios

  • Which Noirelle Makeup foundation shade is best suited for my skin tone?
  • Where can I purchase Noirelle Makeup products locally or online?
  • Are there tutorials or tips for creating a natural everyday makeup look?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme represents modern elegance and femininity.

  • Primary Color: #3B2F2F (Deep Mocha Brown) – used for headers and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #E9A6A6 (Soft Rose Pink) – used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and backgrounds.
  • Accent Color: #FFFFFF (White) – used for text backgrounds and contrast.

Typography

The typography has been selected for readability and elegance.

  • Playfair Display (serif): for headings and titles (luxury, elegant feel).
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): for body text (clean, modern, and highly readable).
